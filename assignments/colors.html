Learning About Colors

The purpose of this page is to provide information on colors.

Primary Colors

A group of colors from which all other colors can be obtained by mixing.

  • Red
  • Blue
  • Green

Secondary Colors

The combinations of two of the three primary colors of light produce the secondary colors of light.

  • Cyan
  • Magenta
  • Yellow

Summary

Red, green, and blue are known as the primary colors of light. The combinations of two of the three primary colors of light produce the secondary colors of light. The secondary colors of light are cyan, magenta, and yellow.
